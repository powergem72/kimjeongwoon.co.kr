Brain network modeling

Brain is a complex network system that people can't understand fully and making network model is still challenging.
The relation between voxels which are small part of brain like pixel cannot be determined because of effects from other voxels.
It means that this kind of connection may not be true because it does not reflect the effect of other areas
But due to number of voxels, it is hard to calculate connection with considering other voxel.
So If I do more research on it, then I will focus on how to reduce the number of voxel properly for making true connection between voxels
